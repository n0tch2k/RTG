About

What started out as a simple compilation of tips for the Steam Workshop became a full on guide to the game Counter-Strike: Global Offensive.

Right now, the guide is counting 26 chapters covering everything from starting the game for the first time, up to becoming a top player.
This guide also collects info on all updates, ranks and everything else you need to know.
